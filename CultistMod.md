## Cults
* **Cult of The Summer**

  * Found in the North

* **Cult of The Fall**
  * 
  * Found in the East

* **Cult of The Winter**
  * Wear Pig/Hoglin skulls
  * Found in the South
  * Guards the Quartz Slate
* **Cult of The Spring**
  * 
  * Found in the West
  * Guards the Diamond Slate
